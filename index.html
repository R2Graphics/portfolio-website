Justin’s Business Card

A modern upgrade for an outdated product, this business card took several attempts to create due to the client’s uncertainty about the exact look he wanted. After trying a few different styles and themes, this is the final product created in the hope of appealing to the strengths of Justin's clients, rather than their intellect.

The Cookie Jar

This was a fun branding project. For a new bakery, I designed a playful logo that used smart typography techniques, an advertisement that drew the attention of potential buyers, a price list that was created to be easy to read and very informative, and much more. This project was conpleted over a few weeks using Adobe Suite- Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
