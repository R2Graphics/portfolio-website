Justin's Business Card

Project Summery

Justin is a Psychologist who was looking for an updated business card. This was his original (see figure1 below)

figure1

At first the client wanted to go for a more abstract look, so I began with using Adobe Illustrator. He showed me a different card I had created for a client of his, and asked me to design something similar (see figure2 below).

figure2

After I created some looks in Illustrator, based on the Jason's brief, he didn't really like any of the samples.

He then realized he wanted something that looked more realistic and natural. He wanted the card to portray an oasis of calmness and serenity. The image of the Cliffs of Moher specifically spoke to him (see figure3 below).

figure3

I then switched programs to Adobe Photoshop, so I could use the image. I used this information to create a visual business card that would portray natural and organic, while appealing to the strengths of his clients, rather than their intellect. I sent the client many variations of the card and he chose the one he liked best. The overall process took 2-3 months because the client lacked the motivation to follow up, and he kept changing his mind for what the overall outcome should be.
